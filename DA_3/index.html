OMG! LAST DAY OF SCHOOL!

  • Originality: This game is an adventure game with a completely original concept of signing yearbooks
  • Technical Merit: My game features a CSV overworld map and multiple tile types, as well as yearbook sprites.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I am having trouble getting states to work for this game. I enjoyed creating the CSV map.
  • Prototype Assets: book.png sprite2.png
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game most closely resembles the Phaser 2 Tile Mapping example
  • High Concept: It's the last day of school! You must sign all the yearbooks before time runs out.
  • Theme: Last day of school.
  • Mandated Variety: The variety comes from the inconsistency of the player, as the game is technically demanding.
  • Prototype Goal: Movement, strategy, precision.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should be excited to learn the best strategies to get to all the yearbooks in time.
  • Gameplay: Use the arrow keys to move your character. Avoid water that clumsy students have spilled on the school floor.
  • Strategies: Ride along walls for an easier time avoiding spilled water.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: A middle schooler on the last day of school.
  • Target Audience: All gamers.
  • Play Time: A minute or less.
