Forest of Doom

  • Originality: This game is a classic maze game with an original horror twist.
  • Technical Merit: My game allows the player to toggle their weapon between flashlight and stick, using the stick to fight NPCs and the flashlight to increase visibility.
  • Prototype Postmortem: It was difficult to find a way to make the game "at night". The black image does not always follow the camera will, especially when the flashlight is off.

  • High Concept: Escape from the Forest of Doom, overcoming enemies and darkness on the way.
  • Theme: Horror, escape.
  • Mandated Variety: NPC activity will vary based on player activity.
  • Prototype Goal: Movement, escape, limited view.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
  • Gameplay: The player moves around in the dark, trying to escape the maze. The Q button toggles between the flashlight and the stick. The stick must be used to fight NPCs, but the flashlight provides an extended field of view. When the player finds their way out of the maze, they win.
  • Strategies: Have the flashlight out most of the time and only use the stick when you see enemies nearby. Try and remember where you are on the map so you don't get lost.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Part of the setting is the player does not know where they are. They are trapped in a dark, scary forest and must make it out. The forest is full of danger.
  • Target Audience: Gamers and horror fans.
  • Play Time: About 5 minutes.
